Oatmeal

This recepie is easy to prepare. Time to prepare ingredients is 5 min, while cooking time is about 10 min.

Ingreadients for two portions

  • 1 cup milk
  • ½ cup water
  • ¾ cup oats
  • ½ cup blueberries
  • ½ cup applesauce
  • 2 tablespoons wheat germ
  • 2 teaspoons ground cinnamon
  • 2 teaspoons sugar

Directions

  1. Bring the milk and water to a boil in a pot
  2. Mix in the oats, and reduce heat to medium
  3. Stir in blueberries, applesauce, wheat germ, cinnamon, and sugar
  4. ook 8 to 10 minutes, or until oats are tender
